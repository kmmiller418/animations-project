It is not by mere coincidence that two of the greatest questions of mankind pertain to the majestic Gallus gallus, family Phasianidae, order Galliformes. Indeed, which came first, the chicken or the egg? And why, why did the chicken cross the road?

Perhaps it is not our prerogative to know. Perhaps instead, we should wonder why it is that the chicken came to be at such a forefront of human marvel, and what we can do to further foster that senser of wonder within ourselves.

It's time to embark on the chicken way of life.

Wise chicken advice: face your fears. Stare them down. If you're in a burning building, stay right there. Take a deep breath. Stand your ground.

Wiser chicken advice: don't take advice from chickens.
